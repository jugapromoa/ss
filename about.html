About AquaCraft Bottles

Leading the way in sustainable water bottle manufacturing since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, AquaCraft Bottles began with a simple mission: to create high-quality, sustainable water bottles that combine functionality with environmental responsibility. What started as a small workshop has grown into a leading manufacturer serving clients worldwide.

Our founder, Michael Anderson, recognized the need for durable, eco-friendly alternatives to single-use plastic bottles. With a background in industrial design and a passion for sustainability, he established AquaCraft with the vision of reducing plastic waste through reusable solutions.

Our Mission

To provide businesses and consumers with premium, sustainable water bottle solutions that promote hydration while reducing environmental impact. We combine innovative design with eco-conscious manufacturing practices.

Our Vision

To become the global leader in sustainable drinkware manufacturing, setting industry standards for quality, innovation, and environmental responsibility while helping eliminate single-use plastic waste.
