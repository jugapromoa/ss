Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ varies by product type: 500 units for stainless steel, 800 for glass, and 1,000 for plastic bottles. Custom projects may have different requirements.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 4-6 weeks after design approval. Rush orders (2-3 weeks) are available for an additional fee.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples for custom projects. Sample fees apply but are credited against your first bulk order.

What countries do you ship to?

We ship worldwide to over 50 countries. Our logistics team handles customs clearance for all international shipments.
